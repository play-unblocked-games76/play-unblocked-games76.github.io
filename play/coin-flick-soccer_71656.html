About Coin Flick Soccer

Finger Flick (a.k.a. Dedoball, Peg Game, or &ldquo;Peteleco&rdquo;) is a fast-paced physics game where you use your finger to flick a coin across the board, bounce off nails, and aim for goals. Known by many names around the world, this classic playground challenge now has a digital twist &mdash; smooth physics, satisfying collisions, and endless chances to test your skill!

How to Play

You must click before the coin and flick to direction.
