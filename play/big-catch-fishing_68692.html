About Big Catch Fishing

In Big Catch Fishing, you play as a cute cat on a fishing adventure! Tap the screen to drop your hook into the water. While it sinks, move it left and right to collect as many fish as you can. When the hook comes back up, tap on the fish to shoot them and earn coins. Use those coins to buy upgrades in the shop. You can go deeper, make your line faster, and unlock better weapons like rifles, shotguns, and even a sniper! There are also special tools like lights and electric shocks to help you catch more.
